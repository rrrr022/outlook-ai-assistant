How do I install the add-in?

In Outlook, go to Get Add-ins ‚Üí My add-ins ‚Üí Add a custom add-in ‚Üí Add from URL, then paste: https://zealous-ground-01f6af20f.2.azurestaticapps.net/manifest.azure.xml

Is my data safe?

Yes! We use a "Bring Your Own Key" model. Your emails go directly from your browser to your chosen AI provider. We never see, store, or process your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Which AI provider should I choose?

GitHub Models (Recommended) - Free if you have a GitHub account. Great for getting started.
OpenAI - Best quality (GPT-4), pay-per-use pricing.
Anthropic Claude - Excellent for longer, thoughtful responses.
xAI Grok - Good for current events, integrated with X/Twitter.

Why isn't the AI responding?

Check that: 1) Your API key is valid and has credits, 2) You've selected the correct provider in Settings, 3) Your API provider's service is operational. You can test your connection in the Settings panel.

How much does it cost?

The add-in itself is free. You only pay for AI API usage based on your provider's pricing. GitHub Models is free for limited use!

Can I use this at work?

Yes! The BYOK model means your organization's data never touches our servers. However, check with your IT department about using third-party add-ins and AI services.

How do I uninstall?

In Outlook, go to Get Add-ins ‚Üí My add-ins, find "Outlook AI", click the three dots (‚ãØ), and select Remove.
